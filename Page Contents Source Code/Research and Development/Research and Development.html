Research and Development


This will mainly involve in conducting research studies associated with enterprise forums, government policies along with monitoring and evaluation of national programmes. The study covers diverse areas pertaining to the micro, small and medium scale entrepreneur development.

  • Research on import substitution
  • Analyse policies related with MSME development
  • Impact assessment of ongoing projects for SME development
  • Maintain updated database for MSMEs
  • Develop training modules for SME development
  • Develop and identify regional/national level plans for entrepreneurship development
